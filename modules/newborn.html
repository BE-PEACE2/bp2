👶 Newborn Care 101

🥛 Feeding Basics

Newborns usually eat every 2–3 hours. Breastfeeding provides the perfect nutrition, but formula is a safe alternative if required.

😴 Sleep Patterns

Most newborns sleep 14–17 hours a day in short stretches. Always place your baby on their back for safe sleep.

🛁 Hygiene & Safety

Keep the umbilical cord stump clean and dry, use mild products, and change diapers frequently to avoid rashes.

💖 Bonding

Skin-to-skin contact, gentle talking, and cuddles help your newborn feel safe and loved.
