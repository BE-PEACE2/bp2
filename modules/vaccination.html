💉 Child Vaccination Guide

Vaccines protect children from life-threatening diseases and are one of the most effective ways to ensure long-term health.

📅 Routine Immunization

Follow the government’s immunization schedule for timely protection against diseases like polio, measles, and hepatitis.

💪 Importance of Timely Doses

Delays in vaccination can leave children vulnerable. Always keep a record of due and received doses.

🧾 Keep Records

Maintain a vaccination card or digital record for easy tracking and school requirements.

👨‍⚕️ Consult Your Pediatrician

Always consult your doctor if you have concerns about vaccine safety or timing.
