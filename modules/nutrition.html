🥦 Child Nutrition Basics

Good nutrition is the foundation for your child’s growth, development, and immunity. Here are the basics every parent should know:

🍼 Breastfeeding & Formula

Breast milk is the gold standard for infants, providing immunity and bonding. If needed, formula feeding is a safe and effective alternative.

🥣 Introducing Solids

Begin complementary feeding at around 6 months. Start with iron-rich foods such as pureed lentils, fortified cereals, or mashed vegetables.

🥗 Balanced Diet

Include a colorful mix of fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and proteins in your child’s daily meals for optimal growth and strong immunity.

💧 Hydration

Water is the healthiest drink. Limit fruit juices and avoid sugary drinks to prevent tooth decay and obesity.
