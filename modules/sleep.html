😴 Healthy Sleep for Children

Sleep is essential for growth, brain development, and emotional well-being. Here’s how to build healthy sleep habits:

🕰 Sleep Duration

Newborns: 14–17 hours/day
Toddlers: 11–14 hours/day
School-age: 9–12 hours/day

🌙 Bedtime Routine

Maintain a calm bedtime ritual — warm bath, reading, dim lights — to help children wind down.

📱 Screen-Free Zone

Avoid screens at least 1 hour before bed to improve sleep quality and melatonin release.

🛏 Safe Sleep

Infants should always sleep on their backs, in a separate crib, without pillows or toys.
