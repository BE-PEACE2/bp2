🩺 Dashboard Help & User Guide

📅 Viewing Your Appointments

All your scheduled and past consultations appear in the dashboard. You can easily join an upcoming meeting or view receipts from past visits.

🔗 Joining a Consultation

To join your online session, simply click the “Join” button next to the upcoming consultation in your dashboard. A secure video room will open automatically.

📜 Viewing Receipts

After a consultation, your receipt can be downloaded using the “Receipt” button under Past Consultations.

⚙️ Logging Out

You can safely log out anytime by clicking the Logout button on the top-right corner of your header. It ensures your session ends securely.
