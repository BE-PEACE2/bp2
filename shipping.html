Shipping Policy

BE PEACE provides online health consultation services. Since our services are delivered digitally through video consultation and email, there is no physical shipping of products.

Upon successful booking and payment, you will receive confirmation and consultation details at your registered email address and/or WhatsApp.

If you do not receive confirmation within 24 hours of booking, please contact us at info@bepeace.in for assistance.
